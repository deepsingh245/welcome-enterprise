High-Performance Onion Slicer for Restaurants and Large Gatherings

Our onion slicer is made for busy kitchens. It has a strong motor that quickly and accurately slices onions, cucumbers, radishes, and other vegetables. Made from high-quality stainless steel, it's built to last and is easy to clean. This slicer is perfect for chefs who need to make perfectly sliced onions for their dishes, especially for the first step of cooking many recipes. It comes in two sizes.

PRODUCT DETAILS

This Slicer is equipped with a stainless steel drum, precision-engineered cutting blades, and a motor shaft made of stainless steel, ensuring a rust-free and hygienic slicing process. It guarantees smooth and efficient slicing every time. Additionally, an inbuilt MCB provides protection against electrical fluctuations, enhancing the safety of the machine.

PRODUCT DESIGN

The Slicer is designed for convenience, offering easy handling and mobility, making it ideal for fast-paced environments. Available in two models, the compact version features a 1 HP motor, while the larger option is fitted with a 1.5 HP motor. Whether you're catering for a large event or running a busy restaurant, this wet slicing is a dependable solution for heavy-duty slicing tasks.
